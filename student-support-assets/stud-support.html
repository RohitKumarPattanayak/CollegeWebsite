STUDENT SUPPORT SYSYTEM

The institution is motivated to cater to the intellectual as well as psychological needs of the students. The students also need to possess the soft skills to face the real competitive world outside. Well planned support system has been adopted to ensure holistic development of the student, teacher and parent. Some of the highlights are :

MERITS
  • Scholarship for meritorious students.
  • Special coaching for competitive entrance exams which is integrated with regular course.
MENTAL TRAINING
  • Mind Power training.
  • Brain gym workshops.
  • Mind mapping and marks card mapping to improvise the learning process.
GUIDANCE
  • Counselling from expert counsellors.
  • Emphasize on the development of soft skills which are essentially required in the competitive world.
SLOW LEARNER SUPPORT
  • Additional classes for slow learners.
  • Workshops on Personality Development.
  • Workshops to improve memory, concentration and career guidance.
ASSESMENTS AND FEEDBACK
  • Regular assessments and tests.
  • Continuous and effective feedback and tips to improvise.
  • Experiance based guidance.
