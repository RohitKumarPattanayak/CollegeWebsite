GENERAL RULES OF THE INSTITUTION

“ Education is the manifestation of divinity already existing in man. "

– Swamy Vivekananda

Indian Philosophy has always looked upon humans as divine souls with perfection already within them. Education is the tool which brings it out and hence the place of learning is regarded as a temple. In order to maintain the sanctity and the sacredness of the institution, students are expected to follow following rules and regulations :

RULES
  • Be punctual to the college, and adhere to the timings given.
  • In case of any absence, prior permission needs to be taken from the concerned authorities. Absence related to medical emergencies needs to be reported duly.
RULES
  • Attend all the regular classes, coaching classes and the remedial classes without fail.
  • 75% attendance is compulsory, failing which the student will not be allowed to take up the final examination.
RULES
  • All the infrastructure facilities, apparatus and machines in the laboratory should be handled with care. It is the duty of every student to respect and protect the property of the Institution.
  • behaviour is regularly monitored.
RULES
  • Ragging, smoking, inappropriate behaviour is strictly prohibited within the college campus. Anybody found violating the rules will face strict disciplinary action against them.
  • uniform is compulsary.
RULES
  • Must follow the dress code prescribed by the institution.
  • Continuous and effective feedback and tips to improvise.
  • College Identity card needs to be carried every day.
